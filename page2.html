WHY NEURO FINANCE?
How do we function?

With more than trillions of TB worth of data in storage and increasing, companies want to make sure that they have all the information needed for suitable analytics which can come to use in future marketing plans.

Banks spend billions to understand their customers, still majority of customer don't take advice from banks when looking for new investments; They prefer to take advice from family members or relatives.

We at NeuroFinance understand the problem statement and we take this financial data and put it in our Deep Learning Algorithm which when processed through Psychometric Rules presents us with an unique customer profile which can be used to understand customer better.


We can basically grasp the heartbeat of current financial trend in country to introduce specific products into economy to boost customers and provide better customer services.
