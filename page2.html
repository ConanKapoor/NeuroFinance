NEURO FINANCE
A step by step demonstration

Here we have used the power of python and user friendliness of Jupyter-Notebook to produce our product Neuro Finance.

Steps followed for the analysis are -

  • First, we took the Prague dataset and cleaned it into a formatted CSV file.
  • We computed more columns from existed data for better analysis and analyzed the data and features for consistency using graphs.
  • Next, we tried fitting the data into different statistical models.
  • After that, the dataframe was normalized for easy analysis.
  • We tried to fit psychological models using model selection and naive_bayes machine learning techniques.
  • Finally, the data was processed to classify every customer into a particular psychological personality.





We can basically grasp the heartbeat of current financial trend in country to introduce specific products into economy to boost customers and provide better customer services.

DATA ANALYSIS
5 psychological personalities

The Anxious Investor
  • Want to be rich
  • Want to be successful
  • Passionate investers
  • Overestimate earnings
  • Underestimate expenses
  • Inhonest about income
  • Unfix financial goals
The Hoarder
  • Worry about money
  • Save a lot of money
  • Never invest all of it
  • Accumulate assets
  • Not much liquid money
  • Risk apetite is low
  • Invest very less
The Cash Splasher
  • Take too much debt
  • High use of credit cards
  • Want instant gratification
  • Limit expenses
  • Underestimate expenses
  • Honest about income
  • Fix financial goal
The Fitbit Financier
  • Don't want to change financial status
  • prefer complacency
  • Never diversify
  • Lose out on oppurtunities
  • No saving plan
Ostrich
  • Confused about money.
  • Embarrassed about money.
  • Guilt about money.
  • Procrastinate.
  • Can't handle money
  • Prefer automatic plans.
