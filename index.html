WHY NEURO FINANCE?
How do we function?

With more than trillions of TB worth of data in storage and increasing, companies want to make sure that they have all the information needed for suitable analytics which can come to use in future marketing plans.

Banks spend billions to understand their customers, still majority of customer don't take advice from banks when looking for new investments; They prefer to take advice from family members or relatives.

We at NeuroFinance understand the problem statement and we take this financial data and put it in our Deep Learning Algorithm which when processed through Psychometric Rules presents us with an unique customer profile which can be used to understand customer better.


We can basically grasp the heartbeat of current financial trend in country to introduce specific products into economy to boost customers and provide better customer services.

Why predictive ananlysis?
How will it help a bank?

Predictive analysis and deep algorithm, although introduced years ago, hasn’t been used properly in banking sector. With NeuroFinance, we Personalize banking.

We get every detail, every movement of an individual and update our model every second, with each transaction and every card swipe. This data is very helpful in predicting the financial way path of an individual which a bank can exploit to -


  • Attract more customers with personalized financial plans.
  • Provide specific products with Specific Keywords.
  • Detect fraudulent customer who might not comply with rules and norms.
  • Can get insight to what product can be introduced next in the economy according to current financial trend.

DATA SET CHOOSEN   -    PKDD'99 Financial Data Set
                                                   (Click to know more)

Here, we have taken 17 fields for our analysis from the dataset -

  • District_ID - Customer living in a particular district.
  • Age - Age of the customer.
  • Gender - Gender of the customer.
  • Account ID - Account ID associated with the customer.
  • Frequency - Frequency of transactions in dataset.
  • Cash Received - Amount of cash credited in account.
  • Cash spent - Amount of cash withdrawn and probably spent.
  • Credit - Amount of money credited in account.
  • Spent - Amount of money withdrawn and probably spent.
  • Saving - Amount of saving done by the customer.










In addition to the above, we have following specific data for each customer too -

  • s_Household - Money spent on household finances.
  • s_Insurance -Money spent on Insurance finances.
  • s_Leasing - Money spent on Leasing finances.
  • s_Loan - Money spent on Loan finances.
  • s_Statements - Credit card statement.
  • c_Interest - Interest paid by customer on different end.
  • c_pension - Pension received by the customer.
